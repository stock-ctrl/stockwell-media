The straight answers.

Why do you need an estimate tool? +

Because most leads never turn into jobs, and the ones that do still cost you a drive across town just to hand over a number. An estimate tool built into your website lets a customer draw their project, pick materials, and see a real price on the spot. You get the lead with the measurements already done, the ones that would go nowhere filter themselves out, and you show up ready to sell instead of ready to measure.

Which trades can you build a tool for? +

A fence estimate tool is a clear example of what's possible. Landscaping, concrete, driveway coating, decks, pressure washing, and roofing all work the same way. The rule of thumb: if a customer can measure it or draw it, we can usually build a tool for it. Ask us about your trade.

Do I have to throw out the tools I already use? +

No. We deploy into the stack you already have and connect to it. The goal is fewer moving parts and less busywork, not a painful migration.

What's the "private client" work about? +

That's a separate, higher-tier service for founders, executives, and firms that run on sensitive data: private data infrastructure, secure briefings, and bespoke internal tooling. It's scoped confidentially and begins with a private conversation. If that's you, reach out directly and we'll talk.

What does it cost? +

It depends on the work. A deployed estimate tool is the entry point; reporting and automation build from there; bespoke engagements are scoped privately. Founding clients lock in our lowest rate. Book a call and we'll give you a straight number.
