About

Built for the trades.
Trusted at the principal level.

Stockwell Media Co is a solo digital studio in Springfield, Missouri. We build instant estimate tools for trade businesses on the ground floor — and design private intelligence and secure infrastructure for principals at the top.

Most agencies sell the same thing to everyone. We started Stockwell to do the opposite — build the right tool for the job, whether that job is a fence quote or a firm's private infrastructure.

It started with the trades. Owner-operators are some of the busiest people there are — quoting jobs, running crews, answering the phone, all before lunch. So we built estimate tools that let their customers measure and price the work themselves, right on the website. The contractor shows up ready to sell instead of ready to measure. The Fencer was the first; more trades are following.

From there, the work compounds. A live tool wants automation and reporting around it. And the same engineering discipline that makes a clean estimator scales all the way up — to principals whose businesses run on sensitive data and high-stakes decisions, who need infrastructure built and operated with real discretion.

So Stockwell works at both ends. On the ground floor: approachable tools that win contractors more jobs. At the top: isolated data environments, executive briefings, and bespoke tooling — designed around how a principal actually operates, with the confidentiality their stakeholders expect. Same studio, same standard, two very different rooms.
